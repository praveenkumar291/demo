starworld

Starworld is an apparel brand owned by an international group which owns the Cotton Club that produces apparel and is a marketer and a vertically-integrated manufacturer. The company and brand's manufacturing centre is in Alexandria, Egypt, and the brand has offices in Warrington (England), Alphen aan den Rijn (Holland) and Nicosia (Cyprus).

History

The brand was first developed in the 1990s and the Cotton Club has sole rights to produce and sell Starworld to the European apparel decoration market. The Cotton Club is a fully certified environmental and ethical factory that is certified WRAP, SMETA, OEKO-TEX 100 Standard and Reach. The factory is also GOTS certified meaning it can produce organic clothing.
This has resulted in Starworld clothing being produced under market leading certifications.
